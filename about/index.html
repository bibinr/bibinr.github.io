About Me

Glad to have piqued your interest in knowing about me or this site. This is a personal site where I hope to write publicly. Not for any particular audience or with any hope of anyone other than me reading it. The idea is to simply write. With the past 6 years of my life, I have come to the understanding and belief that write promotes clarity and writing publicly forces more of it lest someone else happens to read this.

At this site, you will find my ramblings, topics I care about and what I learned about them or what I think about them. All opinions are valid and can change as I learn and discover new ideas. The goal is to learn and improve my own thinking and I do not care about influencing yours.
